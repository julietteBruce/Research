TateOnProducts :: tallyDegrees

tallyDegrees -- collect the degrees of the generators of the terms in a free complex

Synopsis

  • Usage:
    tallyDegrees C
  • Inputs:
    • C, a chain complex, a complex of graded free modules
  • Outputs:
    • a sequence, a sequence of tallies of the degrees of the generators of the free module

Description

Returns for each free module C_d in the complex the result of tally degrees C_d
