diff'(Matrix,Matrix) -- differentiate a matrix by a matrix, the dual notion

Synopsis

  • Function: diff'
  • Usage:
    h = diff'(m,n)
  • Inputs:
    • m, a matrix, a map m : F <--- P between free modules of ranks f and p.
    • n, a matrix, a map n : G <--- Q between free modules of ranks g and q.
  • Outputs:
    • h, a matrix, a matrix with the shape h : F ** dual G <--- P ** dual Q, whose entry in the slot hg*i+j,q*k+l is the result of differentiating nj,l, by mi,k
