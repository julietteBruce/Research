CoherentSheaf ZZ -- canonical twist of a coherent sheaf

Synopsis

  • Operator: SPACE
  • Usage:
    F(n)
  • Inputs:
    • F, a coherent sheaf, or a sheaf of rings, on a projective variety
    • n, an integer
  • Outputs:
    • a coherent sheaf, the twist of F on a projective variety by the n-th power of the hyperplane line bundle.
