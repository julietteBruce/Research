HH_ZZ ChainComplex -- homology of a chain complex

Synopsis

  • Function: homology
  • Usage:
    HH_i C
  • Inputs:
    • i, an integer
    • C, a chain complex
  • Outputs:
    • a module, the homology at the i-th spot of the chain complex C.
