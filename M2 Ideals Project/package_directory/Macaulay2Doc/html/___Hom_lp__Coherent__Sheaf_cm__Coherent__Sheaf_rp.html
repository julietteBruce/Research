Hom(CoherentSheaf,CoherentSheaf) -- global Hom

Synopsis

  • Function: Hom
  • Usage:
    Hom(F,G)
  • Inputs:
    • F, a coherent sheaf
    • G, a coherent sheaf, both should be sheaves on a projective variety or scheme X = Proj R
  • Outputs:
    • a module, over the coefficient field of R

Description

If F or G is a sheaf of rings, it is regarded as a sheaf of modules in the evident way.
