CoherentSheaf / CoherentSheaf -- quotient of coherent sheaves

Synopsis

  • Operator: /
  • Usage:
    F / G
  • Inputs:
    • F, a coherent sheaf
    • G, a coherent sheaf, or an ideal
  • Outputs:
    • a coherent sheaf, the quotient sheaf F/G
