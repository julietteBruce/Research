localComplement -- find the splitting of the target of a map

Synopsis

  • Usage:
    localComplement m
  • Inputs:
    • m, a matrix, representing a homomorphism of free modules over a local ring
  • Outputs:
    • a matrix, the complement of the image of the homomorphism represented by m

Description

The function finds a splitting of the target of m as a direct sum of the image of m and the image of the output.
