NumericSolutions :: jordanForm

jordanForm -- Jordan canonical form

Synopsis

  • Usage:
    jordanForm(A)
  • Inputs:
    • A, a square matrix.
  • Optional inputs:
    • Tolerance => ..., default value .001, numerical tolerance.
  • Outputs:
    • chBasMatr, the matrix that transforms A in Jordan form.
    • sepEigen, the generalized eigenvectors.
    • eigNrep, the eigenvalues of the matrix A.
    • algMult, the algebraic multiplicity of each eigenvalue.
    • jorFor, the Jordan form of A.

Caveat

the procedure involves computation over inexact field and hence the results may be not reliable.
