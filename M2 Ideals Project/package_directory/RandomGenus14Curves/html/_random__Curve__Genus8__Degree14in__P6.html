RandomGenus14Curves :: randomCurveGenus8Degree14inP6

randomCurveGenus8Degree14inP6 -- Compute a random normal curve of genus g=8 and degree 14 in \PP^6

Synopsis

  • Usage:
    I=randomCurveGenus8Degree14inP6 S
  • Inputs:
    • S, a polynomial ring, in 7 variables
  • Outputs:
    • I, an ideal, of a curve of geometric genus 8 and degree 14 in \PP^6

Description

The construction is based on Mukai's unirational description of $M_{8,8}$ of the moduli space of genus 8 with 8 marked points (see [Mu]).

i1 : setRandomSeed("alpha");
 
i2 : FF=ZZ/10007;
 
i3 : S=FF[x_0..x_6];
 
i4 : I=randomCurveGenus8Degree14inP6 S;

o4 : Ideal of S
 
i5 : betti res I

            0 1  2  3  4 5
o5 = total: 1 7 35 56 35 8
         0: 1 .  .  .  . .
         1: . 7  .  .  . .
         2: . . 35 56 35 8

o5 : BettiTally
