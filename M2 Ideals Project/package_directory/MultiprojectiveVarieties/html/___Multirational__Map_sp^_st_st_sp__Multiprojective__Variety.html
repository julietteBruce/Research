MultirationalMap ^** MultiprojectiveVariety -- inverse image via a multi-rational map

Synopsis

  • Operator: ^**
  • Usage:
    Phi^** Y
    Phi^* Y
  • Inputs:
    • Phi, a multi-rational map
    • Y, a multi-projective variety, a subvariety of the ambient multi-projective space of the target of Phi
  • Outputs:
    • a multi-projective variety, the (closure of the) inverse image of Y via Phi
