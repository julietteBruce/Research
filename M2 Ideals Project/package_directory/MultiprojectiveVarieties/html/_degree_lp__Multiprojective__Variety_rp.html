MultiprojectiveVarieties :: degree(MultiprojectiveVariety)

degree(MultiprojectiveVariety) -- the degree of the variety

Synopsis

  • Function: degree
  • Usage:
    degree X
  • Inputs:
    • X, a multi-projective variety
  • Outputs:
    • an integer, the degree of the image of $X$ via the Segre embedding of the ambient of $X$

Description

i1 : X = PP_QQ^({2,1},{1,3});

o1 : ProjectiveVariety, threefold in PP^2 x PP^3
 
i2 : degree X

o2 = 9
