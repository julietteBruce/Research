MultiprojectiveVarieties :: GG(ZZ,MultirationalMap)

GG(ZZ,MultirationalMap) -- induced automorphism of the Grassmannian

Synopsis

  • Function: GG
  • Usage:
    GG(k,f)
  • Inputs:
    • k, an integer, to indicate the Grassmannian GG$(k,n)$ of $k$-dimensional subspaces of $\mathbb{P}^n$
    • f, a multi-rational map, an automorphism of $\mathbb{P}^n$
  • Outputs:
    • a multi-rational map, the induced automorphism of GG$(k,n)$
