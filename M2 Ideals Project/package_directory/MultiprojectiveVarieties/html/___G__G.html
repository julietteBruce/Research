MultiprojectiveVarieties :: GG

GG -- the Grassmannian of k-dimensional linear subspaces of an n-dimensional projective space

Synopsis

  • Usage:
    GG(k,PP_K^n)
    GG_K(k,n)
    GG Grass(k,n,K,Variable=>"x")
  • Inputs:
    • k, an integer
    • P, an embedded projective variety, a projective space of dimension $n$
  • Outputs:
    • a Grassmannian variety, which parametrizes the $k$-dimensional subspaces of $\mathbb{P}^n$
