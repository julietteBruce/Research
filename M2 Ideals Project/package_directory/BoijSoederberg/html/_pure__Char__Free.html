BoijSoederberg :: pureCharFree

pureCharFree -- first betti number of specific exact complex

Synopsis

  • Usage:
    pureCharFree
  • Inputs:
    • a list, a strictly increasing sequence of degrees
  • Outputs:
    • an integer, The zero-th betti number of the corresponding pure resolution construction

Description

pureCharFree corresponds to the construction in math.AC/0712.1843v2, "Betti Numbers of Graded Modules and Cohomology of Vector Bundles", Section 5. 
i1 : L = {0,2,3,9}

o1 = {0, 2, 3, 9}

o1 : List
 
i2 : B = pureBettiDiagram L

            0  1  2 3
o2 = total: 7 27 21 1
         0: 7  .  . .
         1: . 27 21 .
         2: .  .  . .
         3: .  .  . .
         4: .  .  . .
         5: .  .  . .
         6: .  .  . 1

o2 : BettiTally
 
i3 : pureCharFree L

o3 = 56
 
i4 : L1 = {0,3,4,6}

o4 = {0, 3, 4, 6}

o4 : List
 
i5 : B1 = pureBettiDiagram L1

            0 1 2 3
o5 = total: 1 8 9 2
         0: 1 . . .
         1: . . . .
         2: . 8 9 .
         3: . . . 2

o5 : BettiTally
 
i6 : pureCharFree L1

o6 = 5
Thus, for large enough multiples m, m*B occurs as the Betti diagram of a module from the pureCharFree construction
