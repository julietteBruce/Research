Dmodules :: cssExpts

cssExpts -- the exponents of the canonical series solutions of I in the direction of a weight vector

Synopsis

  • Usage:
    cssExpts(I,w)
  • Inputs:
    • I, holonomic ideal in a Weyl algebra D
    • w, List of (generic) weights for I, of length half the number of variables in D
  • Outputs:
    • a list, of exponents of the exponents of the canonical series solutions of I in the direction of (-w,w), as in [SST, Theorem 2.3.11].
