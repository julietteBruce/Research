substitute(ChainComplex,Ring) -- Substitute a chain complex to a new ring.

Synopsis

  • Function: substitute
  • Usage:
    substitute(cc,R)
  • Inputs:
    • cc, a chain complex,
    • R, a ring,
  • Outputs:
    • a chain complex,

Description

i2 : cc=res ideal(x_4*x_3, -x_1*x_2+x_4*z_1);
 
i3 : cs=substitute(cc,QQ[x_1..x_4])
