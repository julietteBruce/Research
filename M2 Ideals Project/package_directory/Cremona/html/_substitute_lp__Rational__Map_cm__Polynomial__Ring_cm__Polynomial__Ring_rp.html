Cremona :: substitute(RationalMap,PolynomialRing,PolynomialRing)

substitute(RationalMap,PolynomialRing,PolynomialRing) -- substitute the ambient projective spaces of source and target

Synopsis

  • Function: substitute
  • Usage:
    sub(phi,R,S)
  • Inputs:
    • phi, a rational map, $\phi:X\subseteq\mathbb{P}^n\dashrightarrow Y\subseteq\mathbb{P}^m$
    • R, a polynomial ring, the coordinate ring of $\mathbb{P}^n$
    • S, a polynomial ring, the coordinate ring of $\mathbb{P}^m$
  • Outputs:
    • a rational map, a rational map isomorphic to the original map such that the ambient of the source is $R$ and the ambient of the target is $S$
