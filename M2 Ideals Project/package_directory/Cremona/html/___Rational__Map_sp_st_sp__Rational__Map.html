RationalMap * RationalMap -- composition of rational maps

Synopsis

  • Operator: *
  • Usage:
    phi * psi
    compose(phi,psi)
  • Inputs:
    • phi, a rational map, $X \dashrightarrow Y$
    • psi, a rational map, $Y \dashrightarrow Z$
  • Outputs:
    • a rational map, $X \dashrightarrow Z$, the composition of phi and psi
