Triplets :: BettiDiagram3(Triplet)

BettiDiagram3(Triplet) -- Betti diagrams of the three pure complexes

Synopsis

  • Function: BettiDiagram3
  • Usage:
    BettiDiagram3 T
  • Inputs:
    • T, an instance of the type Triplet, a degree triplet
  • Outputs:
    • a Betti tally, The Betti diagrams of the three complexes in the triplet of pure complexes with the three given degree sequences.

Description

i1 : T = triplet({1,2,3}, {0,2}, {0,2,3})

o1 = {{1, 2, 3}, {0, 2}, {0, 2, 3}}

o1 : Triplet
 
i2 : BettiDiagram3(T)
       0 1 2            0 1            0 1 2
total: 3 6 2     total: 1 3     total: 2 3 1
    1: 3 6 2         0: 1 .         0: 2 . .
                     1: . 3         1: . 3 1
