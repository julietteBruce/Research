Hadamard :: hadamardProduct

hadamardProduct -- computes the Hadamard product of varieties

Ways to use hadamardProduct :

  • hadamardProduct(Ideal,Ideal) -- Hadamard product of two homogeneous ideals
  • hadamardProduct(List) -- Hadamard product of a list of homogeneous ideals, or points
  • hadamardProduct(List,List) -- Hadamard product of two sets of points

For the programmer

The object hadamardProduct is a method function.
