RelativeCanonicalResolution :: scrollDegrees

scrollDegrees -- Computes the degree of a section of a bundle on the scroll ring corresponding to a polynomial in the Cox ring

Synopsis

  • Usage:
    scrollDegrees(ab',e)
  • Inputs:
    • ab', a list, containing the bidegree of polynomial in the Cox ring
    • e, a list, the type of the scroll
  • Outputs:
    • ab, a list, containing the bidegree on the scroll

Description

This function computes integers a and b such that $H^0(O_{P(E)}(aH+bR))$ corresponds to basis($\{a',b'\}$,Rcox), where Rcox is the Cox ring of the scroll $P(E)$.

See also

  • coxDegrees -- Computes the degree of a polynomial in the Cox ring corresponding to a section of a bundle on the scroll

Ways to use scrollDegrees :

  • "scrollDegrees(List,List)"

For the programmer

The object scrollDegrees is a method function.
