RandomSpaceCurves :: expectedBetti(List,ZZ)

expectedBetti(List,ZZ) -- compute the expected betti table from the Hilbert numerator

Synopsis

  • Function: expectedBetti
  • Usage:
    B=expectedBetti(h,r)
  • Inputs:
    • h, a list, values of the Hilbert function
    • r, an integer, dimension of ambient protective space
  • Outputs:
    • B, a Betti tally, expected Betti table of module with Hilbert function h

Description

i1 : betti expectedBetti({0,0,4,6,3,0,0,0,0},3)

            0  1  2 3 4
o1 = total: 4 10 11 8 3
         2: 4 10  3 . .
         3: .  .  8 2 .
         4: .  .  . 6 3

o1 : BettiTally

Caveat

The Hilbert function has to be given at positions 0 to d+r+1 where d is the regularity of the considered variety

Ways to use this method:
