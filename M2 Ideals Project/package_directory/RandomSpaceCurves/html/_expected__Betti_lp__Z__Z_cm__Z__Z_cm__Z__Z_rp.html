RandomSpaceCurves :: expectedBetti(ZZ,ZZ,ZZ)

expectedBetti(ZZ,ZZ,ZZ) -- compute the expected betti table from the Hilbert numerator

Synopsis

  • Function: expectedBetti
  • Usage:
    B=expectedBetti(g,r,d)
  • Inputs:
    • g, an integer, the genus
    • r, an integer, dimension of $\PP^{ r}$
    • d, an integer, the degree
  • Outputs:
    • B, a Betti tally, a Betti table that has Hilbert numerator the same as for a nondegenerate maximal-rank curve of genus g and degree d in $\PP^{ r}$, with O_C(2) non-special.

Description

i1 : betti expectedBetti(0,4,4)

            0 1 2 3
o1 = total: 1 6 8 3
         0: 1 . . .
         1: . 6 8 3

o1 : BettiTally
