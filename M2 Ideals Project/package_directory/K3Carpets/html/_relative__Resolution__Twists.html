K3Carpets :: relativeResolutionTwists

relativeResolutionTwists -- compute the twists in the relative resolution

Synopsis

  • Usage:
    L = relativeResolutionTwists(am,bm,F)
  • Inputs:
    • am, an integer,
    • bm, an integer,
    • F, a chain complex,
  • Outputs:
    • L, a list, of Lists of multidegrees

Description

We compute the twists of the relative resolution in the resonance scroll of a degenerate K3 X_e(a,b) in case of k resonance after re-embedding the resonance scroll with |H+jR| for j=am-a_0=bm-b_0 where $\{a_i\}|\{b_j\}$ is the splitting type of the resonance scroll.
