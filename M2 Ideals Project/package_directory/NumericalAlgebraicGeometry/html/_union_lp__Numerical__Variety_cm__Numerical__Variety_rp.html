NumericalAlgebraicGeometry :: union(NumericalVariety,NumericalVariety)

union(NumericalVariety,NumericalVariety) -- union of numerical varieties

Synopsis

  • Function: union
  • Usage:
    VW=union(V,W)
  • Inputs:
    • V, a numerical variety
    • W, a numerical variety
  • Outputs:
    • VW, a numerical variety

Description

Constructs the union of numerical varieties

Caveat

The resulting numerical variety may have redundant components.

See also

  • removeRedundantComponents -- remove redundant components

Ways to use this method:

  • union(NumericalVariety,NumericalVariety) -- union of numerical varieties
